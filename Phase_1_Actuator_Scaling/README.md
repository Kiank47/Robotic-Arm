# Phase 1 Funding Proposal: Actuator Scaling and Synchronization

## 1. Project Objective
To construct two additional high-torque, precision actuators. These will be combined with an existing, successfully tested actuator to form the foundational 3-axis base of a 6-DOF collaborative robotic arm.

## 2. Proof of Concept & Risk Mitigation
The core actuator design has already been built and successfully validated. The actuator has been tested and achieved an accuracy of less than 15 arc seconds, because I cannot measure any lower. The current working unit successfully integrates an ODrive controller, a high-torque brushless motor, and a harmonic drive. Funding these two additional units carries near-zero R&D risk regarding the component stack, as the hardware combination is proven to function effectively.

## 3. Bill of Materials (Requested Funding)
This request covers only the required electronics and mechanical drives for the two new joints. Structural 3D printed components and the necessary magnetic encoders have already been sourced independently.

* **2x ODrive S1 Motor Controllers**: $149.99 each, totaling $299.98. Specifications require screw terminals. The vendor is ODrive.
* **2x Flipsky 6354 Brushless Motors**: $71.00 each, totaling $142.00. Specifications require an 8mm round short shaft, 190Kv, and without pulley. The vendor is Flipsky.
* **2x HBS17-100 Harmonic Drives**: $59.51 each, totaling $119.02. Specifications require HBS-17 and a reduction ratio of 100. The vendor is AliExpress.

**Total Requested Funding for Phase 1: $561.00**
